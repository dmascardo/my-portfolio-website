Persona: Jastine Hazel

Purpose

This website is my professional portfolio that I would use to show my skills to employers, and highlights the projects I have completed

Audience

My site would be for people in the technology industry like startup founders, hiring managers, recruiters, and other developers, who may want to learn about my work

Goal

My main goal is to make it easy to understand for future employers or hiring managers to contact or learn more about my professional skills. Another goal is to display my projects and skills in a clean and professional way to support my online presence

Overview

Name: Jastine Hazel

Age: 30

Profession: Tech Recruiter at Code4Hire

Code4Hire is a Dallas-based tech company that specializes in connecting startups and mid-sized businesses with new junior full-stack developer applicants who are eager to grow their knowledge and careers

Technology

  • Windows 11 laptop
  • Iphone 15 pro max for LinkedIn/Slack/Email

Attidudes and Behaviors

  • Busy, fast-paced, and workaholic
  • Spends less than two minutes scanning a candidate's portfolio website
  • Her point of view goes straight to the projects section, technical skills, and contact information
  • Prefers sites that are polished, easy to navigate, and mobile-friendly
  • Checks on Github and LinkedIn links before deciding whether to forward the candidate to a hiring manager

Frustrations and Needs

  • She needs to identify qualified candidates quickly because she manages dozens of job requisitions at once
  • Frustrated by cluttered or outdated portfolio websites, missing contact details, and vague skills descriptions
  • She needs to know at a GLANCE whether a candidate has the skills her clients are looking for (clean code practices, teamwork, and adaptability

Goals

  • Her main goal is to decide whether this portfolio owner is a serious and competitive candidate worth recommending for an interview

    • She wants to confirm within a couple of minutes whether:

  • Candidate has relevant technical skills, completed projects that showcase problem-solving ability, and site provides clear, professional ways to contact the candidate

User Story

    As a Tech Recruiter, she needs to quickly determine if this candidate has the right skills and portfolio projects to recommend for a client interview so that she do not waste her time reviewing unqualified applicants
